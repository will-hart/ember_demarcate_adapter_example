Ember.js and Demarcate.js

This is a quick demonstration of the power of Ember.js to integrate with other javascript libraries and provide reusable interface elements in the form of Views and Components. The details of the implementation are discussed in a blog post, but in short a view or component is converted into a demarcate.js editor, and the markdown is pushed into a bound property as you type.

You can have a look at a demo of the Ember.View based approach or the Ember.Component based approach.
